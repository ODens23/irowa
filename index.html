Approach To
Health care

At Irowa Medical Centre, we believe that providing high quality, accessible health care is our reason for being. We seek to be a model for other community health centers. We care for the whole person, see the complexity of each person’s life, and believe that addressing a broad range of human needs is the best way to improve a person’s health.

We continuously examine the services we provide and what is needed in the community. We use a team approach to providing health care, and involve the patient as part of our team.

Mission

To provide patient-centered Healthcare with excellence in quality, service and access.

Vision

Irowa Medical Centre enhance the lives of the people we serve by providing access to high value, patient-centered care in collaboration with those who share our values.

Values

Our relationship with the people we serve; we are dedicated to patient care.
