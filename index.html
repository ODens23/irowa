Approach To
Health care

At Irowa Medical Centre, we believe that providing high quality, accessible health care is our reason for being in the health care industry. We seek to be a model for other community health centers. We care for the whole person, see the complexity of each person’s life, and believe that addressing a broad range of human health needs is the best way to improve a person’s health.

We continuously examine the services we provide and what is needed in the community. We use a team approach to providing health care, and involve the patient as part of our team.
