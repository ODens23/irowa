Specialist Service

If specialist care is what you need, then you have come to the right place because Irowa Medical Centre has specialists with decades of experience who will take care of your health needs in a professional manner. We also understand that every patient's healthcare need is unique. That's why we offer a range of specialist services designed to address specific medical conditions and provide specialized care to those who require a higher level of expertise and attention. Our specialist services are staffed by experienced doctors and healthcare professionals who are dedicated to providing the highest quality of care to our patients. Our Medical Centre is affiliated with other specialized medical facilities which are not readily available within the four walls of our Medical Centre; which include Dental Services, Ear, Nose, and Throat (E.N.T), and Eye care services.


We deliver the following specialist services;

1. Family Medicine
Family medicine is a medical specialty that provides comprehensive and continuous healthcare for individuals and families across all ages, genders, and diseases. Family medicine physicians are trained to diagnose and treat a wide range of acute and chronic illnesses, manage complex medical problems, and provide preventive care to promote overall health and well-being. Our Medical facility, we are committed to providing exceptional family medicine services to our patients. Our team of highly trained and experienced family medicine physicians works closely with patients to develop customized treatment plans that meet their unique healthcare needs. Whether you are dealing with a chronic illness, managing multiple health conditions, or simply seeking preventive care, our family medicine team is here to help you achieve and maintain optimal health.

2. Emergency Medical Care
Emergency medical care is a crucial component of any healthcare system, and our facility is proud to offer exceptional emergency medical services to our patients. Our emergency department is staffed by a team of highly trained and experienced healthcare professionals who are equipped to handle a wide range of medical emergencies, from minor injuries to life-threatening conditions. Our emergency medical care services are available 24/7, ensuring that patients receive prompt and efficient care when they need it most. Our team works closely with other departments within the facility, including our imaging and laboratory services, to provide rapid and accurate diagnoses and treatment plans. At Irowa Medical Centre, we understand that medical emergencies can be stressful and overwhelming for patients and their families. That's why we are committed to providing compassionate and personalized care that meets the unique needs of each patient.

3. Cardiology
Our cardiology department provides comprehensive care for patients with heart diseases. Our team of cardiologists uses the latest diagnostic tools and treatment techniques to manage heart conditions such as coronary artery disease, arrhythmias, and heart failure. We also offer interventional cardiology services such as angioplasty and stent placement, as well as cardiac rehabilitation programs to help patients recover and improve their heart health.

4. Oncology
Our oncology department provides advanced cancer treatment services to patients diagnosed with various types of cancer. Our team of medical oncologists, radiation oncologists, and surgical oncologists work together to develop individualized treatment plans tailored to each patient's specific needs. We offer a range of cancer treatments, including radiation therapy, chemotherapy, and surgical procedures, as well as supportive care services such as pain management and palliative care.

5. Neurology
Our neurology department provides diagnosis, treatment, and management of neurological disorders such as stroke, epilepsy, Parkinson's disease, multiple sclerosis, and other conditions that affect the nervous system. Our neurologists use the latest diagnostic tools and treatment techniques, including imaging studies and neurostimulation therapies, to help our patients achieve the best possible outcomes.

6. Orthopedics
Our orthopedic department provides comprehensive care for musculoskeletal conditions and injuries. We offer a wide range of services, including joint replacement surgery, sports medicine, treatment for spine and back injuries, and rehabilitation programs. Our team of orthopedic surgeons, physical therapists, and other healthcare professionals work together to develop individualized treatment plans that help our patients get back to their daily activities as soon as possible.

7. Pediatrics
Our pediatrics department provides specialized care for infants, children, and adolescents. Our pediatricians offer a full range of services, including neonatal care, pediatric surgery, and pediatric oncology. We also provide preventive care services, including vaccinations, to help keep our young patients healthy.


Obstetrics & Gynecology

Obstetrics and gynecology (OB/GYN), is a medical specialty that focuses on women's reproductive health. At Irowa Medical Centre, we are dedicated to providing exceptional OB/GYN care to women of all ages, from adolescence to menopause. Our team of board-certified OB/GYN physicians is highly trained and experienced in diagnosing and treating a wide range of women's health issues, including pregnancy and childbirth, menstrual disorders, fertility problems, and gynecologic cancers. We offer a full range of services, including routine gynecologic exams, prenatal care, high-risk pregnancy management, minimally invasive surgeries, and menopause management. We understand that women's health needs are unique and can vary throughout their lives. That's why we take a personalized approach to care, working closely with each patient to develop customized treatment plans that meet their individual needs and goals. Our team is committed to providing compassionate and supportive care that empowers women to take charge of their health and well-being. Whether you are seeking routine gynecologic care, prenatal care, or treatment for a specific women's health issue, our OB/GYN team is here to provide you with the highest quality of care in a comfortable and supportive environment.

8. Women's Health
Our women's health department offers specialized care for women of all ages. We provide a wide range of services, including obstetrics and gynecology, breast care, and reproductive health services. Our team of obstetricians, gynecologists, and other healthcare professionals work together to provide personalized care that addresses the unique needs of women.

9. Mental Health
Our mental health department provides diagnosis, treatment, and management of mental health conditions. We offer a range of services, including therapy, counseling, and medication management, to help patients achieve optimal mental health. Our mental health professionals work closely with patients to develop individualized treatment plans that address their specific needs and goals.

In addition to the above services, we also offer specialist services such as gastroenterology, urology, pulmonology, and infectious diseases. Irowa Medical Centre is committed to providing the highest quality of care to our patients. If you require specialized care, please contact us to learn more about our specialist services and to schedule an appointment with one of our healthcare professionals.
