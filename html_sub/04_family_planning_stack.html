Healthy Family Planning

At Irowa Medical Centre, we give Professional advice on the methods, Health benefits, and processes of family planning. Use of contraception prevents pregnancy-related health risks for women, girls of adolescent ages, and when child births are systematically separated by less than two years, the infant mortality rate is 45% higher than it is when births are 2-3 years inbetween and 60% higher when births are four or more years apart.
