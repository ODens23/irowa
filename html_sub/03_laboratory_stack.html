Laboratory Diagnostics

Our state-of-the-art laboratory provides a wide range of services to support your healthcare needs. We are committed to providing accurate, reliable and timely laboratory results to help your healthcare providers make informed decisions about your care.

Our laboratory is staffed by highly trained and experienced professionals who utilize the latest technology and equipment to perform a variety of tests. We offer a comprehensive range of laboratory services, including:

1. Hematology
We provide testing for full blood count including; red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets to diagnose and monitor a range of conditions such as anemia, infection, and cancer. Also, other wide range of hematological test are accessible in our laboratory.

2. Clinical Chemistry
We provide testing for a range of biochemical markers to diagnose and monitor conditions such as liver and kidney disease, diabetes, and heart disease.

3. Microbiology
We provide testing for infectious agents such as bacteria, viruses, and fungi to diagnose and treat infectious diseases.

4. Immunology
We provide testing to diagnose and monitor conditions such as allergies, autoimmune diseases, and cancer.

5. Blood Banking
We provide blood and blood products for transfusion and the products are adequately screened to ensure the safety and compatibility of donated blood.

6. Pathology testing
We also offer specialized services such as genetic testing, cytology, histology, and another pathological test as maybe necessary.

Our laboratory is accredited by the appropriate regulatory agencies, we participate in quality control, quality assurance, and proficiency testing programs to ensure the accuracy and reliability of our results. We understand the importance of timely results, and we strive to provide results as quickly as possible without compromising accuracy.

At Irowa Medical Centres, we are committed to providing the highest quality laboratory services to support your healthcare needs. If you have any questions about our laboratory services, please don't hesitate to contact us. We look forward to serving you!
